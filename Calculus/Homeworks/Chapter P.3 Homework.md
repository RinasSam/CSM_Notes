## Q5:

> Evaluate the function $g(x) = 5 - x^2$ for the given values and simplify.

1. $g(0) = 5 - 0^2 = 5$
2. $g(\sqrt{5}) = 5 - (\sqrt{5})^2 = 5-5 = 0$
3. $g(-2) = 5 - (-2)^2 = 5-4 = 1$
4. $g(t-1) = 5 - (t-1)^2 = 5 -(t^2 - 2t + 1) = 5 - t^2 + 2t - 1 = -t^2 + 2t + 4$
